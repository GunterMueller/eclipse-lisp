Collections

  • The ACM has a searchable table of contents of all ACM jorunals for the past 10 years. Is is updated monthly. Much of what follows, below, is from the ACM index.
  • Henry Baker's archive of research papers (many relevant to Lisp)
  • Kent Pitman's "Parenthetically Speaking" columns from Lisp Pointers.
  • CMU has a collection of papers on garbage collection and memory management.
  • The MIT AI lab and Laboratory for Computer Science LCS/AI Reading Room have many papers on Lisp.
  • Publications of the OOPS Group at. U. Texas includes an extensive survey of garbage collection techniques.
  • This site lists a number of references that went into the formation of the ANSI Standard and books on Lisp.
