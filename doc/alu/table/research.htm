"...where no one has gone before...???"
Whatever you are interested in, the chances are good that someone has done it, is doing it, or has thought about doing it long enough to publish papers on it.

It is very hard to categorize such ever-changing information, but we do list:

  • Some On-line or ftp'able papers involving Lisp.
  • Some universities and other research groups doing work in or on Lisp.
You might also want to look up the authors of the various Lisp tools and examine links to other resources.
